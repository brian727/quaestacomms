About Quaesta

Founded in 2008, Quaesta Instruments, LLC specializes in radiation detection, data logging and communications, remote/unattended monitoring and system integration. We not only offer off-the-shelf neutron and gamma radiation detection sensors but custom designed products as well.

Beyond provding sensors and related to support to partners suchs as the Los Alamos National Laboratory and the International Atomic Energy Agency, Questa also is developing a network of soil moisture probes throughout the U.S. using a unique, non-invasive cosmic ray method.
